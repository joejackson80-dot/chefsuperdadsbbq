Our Story

Born in the Smoke, Raised in North Omaha.

Cameron Lollar isn't just a pitmaster; he's a steward of North Omaha's rich culinary traditions. Founded in 2018, Chef SuperDad's BBQ & Catering was born from a passion for slow-cooked meats and the community that gathers around them.

From backyard gatherings to large-scale corporate events, Cameron brings the same level of craft and care to every plate. It's about more than just food—it's about the heritage of North Omaha and the unbeatable flavor of real wood smoke.
