Our Story

Born in the Smoke, Raised in North Omaha.

Cameron Lollar isn't just a pitmaster — he's a product of North Omaha, one of the most culturally rich communities in the Midwest. Born and raised right here in Nebraska, Cameron discovered his passion for slow-smoked BBQ through family, community, and fire. In 2018 he turned that passion into Chef SuperDad's BBQ & Catering, a full-service catering operation that has since become one of Omaha's most sought-after names for events large and small.

From backyard birthday parties to corporate luncheons, church fundraisers to wedding receptions, Cameron shows up with the same dedication every time — real wood smoke, real seasoning, and real care. His menu draws from the deep well of American BBQ tradition: slow-smoked brisket, fall-off-the-bone ribs, hand-pulled pork, smoked wings, jambalaya, dirty rice, and sides that make people go back for thirds.

Serving Omaha NE and surrounding areas, Chef SuperDad's BBQ & Catering brings the full experience to your event — setup, service, and cleanup. Cameron will personally call you back within 24 hours of your quote request to build a custom menu that fits your crowd and budget. This is North Omaha's BBQ. Come eat.

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book?
Cameron recommends booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for smaller events and 4–6 weeks for large events like weddings or corporate gatherings.
How much BBQ do I need per person?
A general rule is 1/3 lb of cooked meat per adult for a meal with sides. Cameron will help you calculate the right amount during your quote call.
Do you offer delivery or drop-off catering?
Yes. Delivery is available for a flat $15 fee. Buffet-style full-service setup is also available.
What areas do you serve?
Chef SuperDad's BBQ & Catering serves Omaha, Bellevue, Papillion, La Vista, Council Bluffs, and the greater metro area.
How do I get a quote?
Fill out the quote request form above. Cameron will call you back personally within 24 hours.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Yes. Mention any dietary needs in your quote request and Cameron will work with you on the menu.
